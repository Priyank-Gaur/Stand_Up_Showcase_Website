Maheep Singh

Maheep Singh is a renowned Indian stand-up comedian, writer, and actor celebrated for his unique comedic style. Born in 1975 in Patiala, Punjab, he earned a Master’s degree in English Literature from Himachal Pradesh University. Starting his comedy career at 40 to cope with depression, Maheep discovered stand-up through events at Café Rati in Delhi’s India Habitat Centre. Inspired by comedian Apoorv Gupta, he performed for the first time at a comedy show in Greater Kailash, New Delhi. Since then, he has captivated audiences with his engaging performances and relatable humor, marking his place in the Indian comedy scene.

Maheep Singh, founder of the New Delhi Comedy Club (2013), co-won Comedy Central's The Best Stand-up Comedian with Zakir Khan. He has acted in Baked (2015) and Jaspal (2024) and is a playwright, penning works like Nail Polish, Table for Two, and Heer Ke Waris.
