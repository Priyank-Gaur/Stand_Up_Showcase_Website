Vipul Goyal

Vipul Goyal is a highly celebrated Indian stand-up comedian, writer, and actor, best known for his witty humor and sharp observational comedy. A graduate from IIT Bombay, Vipul transitioned from engineering to comedy, becoming one of India's most prominent stand-up acts. He gained widespread fame with his hit web series Humorously Yours on TVF, where he plays a fictionalized version of himself, offering a hilarious glimpse into the life of a comedian.

Vipul’s comedy revolves around everyday life, middle-class struggles, and relatable experiences, delivered with his trademark deadpan style and effortless wit. His ability to craft humor out of ordinary situations has resonated with audiences across all age groups. Vipul has performed sold-out shows across India and internationally, earning a loyal fan base. Known as “The Indian Seinfeld,” Vipul continues to charm audiences with his unique humor, balancing stand-up, acting, and creating content that reflects modern Indian life.
