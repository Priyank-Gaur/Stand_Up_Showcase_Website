Harsh Gujral

Harsh Gujral is a popular Indian stand-up comedian known for his charismatic stage presence, sharp wit, and relatable humor. Born and raised in Kanpur, Uttar Pradesh, Harsh brings a unique flavor to his comedy, blending regional charm with universal appeal. His humor often revolves around middle-class life, family dynamics, societal quirks, and the idiosyncrasies of Indian culture. Harsh gained widespread recognition with his viral stand-up videos on YouTube, which have amassed millions of views. His observational humor and ability to connect with audiences make his performances both hilarious and engaging. Known as "Sasta Vicky Kaushal" by fans for his resemblance to the Bollywood actor, Harsh uses self-deprecating humor and witty improvisation to captivate audiences.

With a growing fan base, sold-out shows across India, and a knack for storytelling, Harsh continues to establish himself as one of the most relatable and entertaining comedians in the Indian comedy scene.
