Anubhav Singh Bassi

Anubhav Singh Bassi is an Indian actor, stand-up comedian, and YouTuber born on January 9, 1991, in Meerut, Uttar Pradesh. A law graduate from Dr. Ram Manohar Lohiya National Law University, he initially pursued careers in UPSC preparation and entrepreneurship before venturing into comedy. Bassi began his stand-up journey in 2017 and has since gained immense popularity, amassing over 200 million views on YouTube and 4 million subscribers. He also has 3 million+ Instagram followers.

Bassi has toured over 35 Indian cities, delivered a TED Talk on his struggles, and featured in a cameo for ZEE5's Comedy Couple (2020).
