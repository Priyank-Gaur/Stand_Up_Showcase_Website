Abhishek Upmanyu

Abhishek Upmanyu is a renowned Indian stand-up comedian, writer, and content creator known for his sharp wit and observational humor. His comedy revolves around everyday situations, social norms, and quirky personal experiences, which he presents with a unique blend of sarcasm and relatable storytelling. Abhishek gained widespread recognition with his performances on popular comedy platforms like Comicstaan and Amazon Prime Video’s Comedy Upbringing.

His debut stand-up special, Thoda Saaf Bol, released on Amazon Prime, showcased his distinct comedic style and earned him praise for his ability to turn the mundane into a hilarious experience. Abhishek’s humor often delves into the intricacies of relationships, family dynamics, and life in metropolitan cities, making his comedy highly relatable to a wide audience. With a growing fan base across India, Abhishek continues to leave a lasting impression with his observational humor, quick wit, and energetic stage presence.
