Aakash Gupta

Aakash Gupta is a celebrated Indian stand-up comedian, actor, and content creator known for his unique style of observational and physical comedy. He rose to fame as the co-winner of Amazon Prime’s Comicstaan Season 2, impressing audiences with his witty performances and dynamic stage presence. Aakash's comedy draws from everyday life, social peculiarities, and his own amusing experiences, delivered with energetic expressions and a distinctive flair.

He has performed in numerous cities across India, earning a loyal fanbase for his relatable humor and engaging storytelling. Aakash’s YouTube stand-up specials, such as Excuse Me Brother, have garnered millions of views, cementing his place as a household name in Indian comedy. Beyond stand-up, he is an active social media entertainer, often sharing funny sketches and content with his followers. Aakash continues to innovate in the comedy scene, captivating audiences with his originality and infectious energy.
